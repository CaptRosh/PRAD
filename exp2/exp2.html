def plot_faces(images,titles,h,w,rows=3,cols=4):
  plt.figure(figsize=(10,10))
  for j in range(rows*cols):
    plt.subplot(rows,cols,j+1)
    plt.imshow(images[j].reshape((h,w)),cmap='gray')
    plt.title(titles[j])
    plt.xticks(())
    plt.yticks(())
  
def titles(y_pred,y_test,names):
  for j in range(y_pred.shape[0]):
    pred_names = names[y_pred[j]].split(' ')[-1]
    actual_names = names[y_test[j]].split(' ')[-1]
    yield f'Predicted: {pred_names}\nActual: {actual_names}'

Conclusion

  1. For the given iris dataset, hard coding of PCA was done, 4 dimensions were converted to 1 dimension, all 150 samples were projected on 1 dimension, got good seperability for one class, and worse seperability for the other 2 classes.
  2. PCL is applied to LFW dataset by choosing top 100 principal components. Projected samples were classified using neural network which gives 90% accuracy for 100 min faces, and 93% for mn faces = 75.
